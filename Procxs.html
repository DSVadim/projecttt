Iphone xs

    Display: The iPhone XS features a 5.8-inch Super Retina OLED display with a resolution of 2436 x 1125 pixels. The display offers exceptional color accuracy, deep blacks, and high contrast for an immersive viewing experience.
    Design: The iPhone XS showcases a sleek and premium design with a glass front and back and a stainless steel frame. It has a notch at the top of the display for Face ID and minimal bezels, providing a modern and elegant look.
    Camera: The iPhone XS boasts a dual 12-megapixel rear camera system with Smart HDR and advanced computational photography capabilities. It captures stunning photos with improved low-light performance and supports portrait mode with adjustable depth control. The front-facing TrueDepth camera enables Face ID and supports portrait selfies and Animoji.
    Performance: Equipped with the A12 Bionic chip, the iPhone XS delivers impressive performance and efficiency. It features a Neural Engine for AI-related tasks and augmented reality experiences. The device runs on iOS, Apple's powerful and user-friendly operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone XS is available in 64GB, 256GB, and 512GB storage options, allowing users to choose the capacity that suits their needs. It does not support expandable storage through a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone XS supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also offers fast 4G LTE Advanced and Gigabit-class LTE for reliable and speedy internet connections.
    Battery: The iPhone XS features a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery that provides all-day usage. It supports fast charging, allowing you to charge the device up to 50% in approximately 30 minutes with a compatible charger. Wireless charging is also supported.
