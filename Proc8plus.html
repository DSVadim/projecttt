Iphone 8+

    Display: The iPhone 8 Plus features a larger 5.5-inch Retina HD display with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. It utilizes IPS LCD technology, offering vibrant colors, excellent brightness, and wide viewing angles.
    Design: Similar to the iPhone 8, the iPhone 8 Plus sports a glass back and aluminum frame, giving it a premium look. It has a sleek and slim design with rounded edges and a Home button with Touch ID for secure authentication.
    Camera: The iPhone 8 Plus boasts a dual-camera system. It includes two 12-megapixel rear cameras with wide-angle and telephoto lenses. The dual cameras enable optical zoom, portrait mode, and depth-of-field effects. The front-facing camera is a 7-megapixel camera for high-quality selfies and FaceTime calls.
    Performance: Powered by the A11 Bionic chip, the iPhone 8 Plus delivers exceptional performance and efficiency. It can handle multitasking, resource-intensive apps, and augmented reality experiences seamlessly. The device runs on iOS, Apple's intuitive and secure operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 8 Plus offers ample storage options, including 64GB and 256GB variants. It does not support expandable storage, so you cannot add additional storage using a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 8 Plus supports a wide range of connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also provides fast 4G LTE connectivity for speedy internet browsing and streaming.
    Battery: The iPhone 8 Plus is equipped with a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery that provides long-lasting usage. It offers similar battery life to the iPhone 8 and supports fast charging and wireless charging for added convenience.
