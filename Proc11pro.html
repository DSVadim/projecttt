Iphone 11 pro

    Display: The iPhone 11 features a 6.1-inch Liquid Retina HD display with a resolution of 1792 x 828 pixels. It utilizes advanced LCD technology to deliver vibrant colors and sharp image quality, providing an immersive viewing experience.
    Design: The iPhone 11 has a stylish and durable design with glass front and back panels reinforced by aluminum or stainless steel frames, depending on the model. It has a sleek look with rounded edges and a notch at the top for Face ID technology.
    Camera: With its dual-camera system, the iPhone 11 excels in photography and video recording. It consists of a 12-megapixel wide-angle lens and a 12-megapixel ultra-wide-angle lens, allowing for capturing a wide field of view. Features such as Night mode, Portrait mode, and 4K video recording are supported.
    Performance: Thanks to the A13 Bionic chip, the iPhone 11 delivers impressive performance and energy efficiency. It incorporates a neural engine for machine learning tasks, enhancing the augmented reality experience and overall device performance. The operating system is iOS, Apple's intuitive operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 11 is available in different storage options, including 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB, allowing for storing a large number of photos, videos, apps, and files. It does not support expandable storage via memory cards.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 11 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also offers fast 4G LTE Advanced and Gigabit-class LTE for speedy internet connections.
    Battery: The iPhone 11 is equipped with a built-in lithium-ion battery that provides long-lasting battery life. It supports fast charging, enabling quick charging with a compatible charger. Wireless charging is supported as well.
    Additional Features: The iPhone 11 includes features such as Face ID for secure facial recognition, stereo speakers for immersive sound, and IP68 rating for water and dust resistance. Dual SIM (nano-SIM and eSIM) is also supported for convenient use of multiple phone numbers.
