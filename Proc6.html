Iphone 6

    Display: The iPhone 6 features a 4.7-inch Retina HD display with a resolution of 1334 x 750 pixels. It offers excellent color accuracy, sharpness, and wide viewing angles, thanks to its IPS LCD technology.
    Design: The iPhone 6 has a sleek and slim design with an aluminum unibody construction. It features rounded edges and a seamless design, giving it a premium look and feel.
    Camera: The iPhone 6 sports an 8-megapixel rear camera with advanced features like autofocus, image stabilization, and HDR. It can capture high-quality photos and record Full HD 1080p videos. The front-facing camera is a 1.2-megapixel camera capable of FaceTime video calls and selfies.
    Performance: Powered by the Apple A8 chip, the iPhone 6 delivers fast and efficient performance. It is capable of handling demanding tasks and running graphics-intensive apps and games smoothly. The device runs on iOS, Apple's operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 6 is available in different storage capacities, including 16GB, 64GB, and 128GB. It does not support expandable storage, so the available storage cannot be expanded with a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 6 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also has 4G LTE capability for fast internet browsing and download speeds.
    Battery: The iPhone 6 is equipped with a non-removable lithium-ion battery. It offers decent battery life, providing up to 14 hours of talk time on 3G, up to 11 hours of video playback, and up to 50 hours of audio playback.
