    Display: The iPhone 10 (iPhone X) features a 5.8-inch Super Retina HD display with a resolution of 2436 x 1125 pixels. The display utilizes OLED technology, offering vibrant colors, deep blacks, and high contrast.
    Design: The iPhone 10 has a stunning design with a glass front and back and a stainless steel frame. It introduces a bezel-less display with a notch at the top, providing an immersive viewing experience.
    Camera: The iPhone 10 boasts a dual 12-megapixel rear camera system with optical image stabilization. It supports portrait mode and portrait lighting effects for professional-looking photos. The front-facing TrueDepth camera enables Face ID facial recognition and captures high-quality selfies.
    Performance: Powered by the A11 Bionic chip with a neural engine, the iPhone 10 delivers exceptional performance and handles resource-intensive tasks effortlessly. It runs on iOS, Apple's advanced mobile operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 10 is available in 64GB and 256GB storage options, providing ample space for apps, photos, and videos. However, it does not support expandable storage via a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 10 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also offers fast 4G LTE Advanced and Gigabit-class LTE for speedy internet connections.
    Battery: The iPhone 10 features a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery that provides all-day battery life. It supports wireless charging, allowing you to conveniently charge the device by placing it on a compatible charging pad.
