Ipad 6

    Display: iPad 6 features a 9.7-inch display with a resolution of 2048 x 1536 pixels. It utilizes IPS LCD technology and delivers vibrant colors and excellent image quality.
    Design: The iPad 6 has a sleek and slim design with a unibody aluminum construction. It features rounded edges and a smooth finish, giving it a premium look and feel.
    Performance: Powered by an Apple A10 Fusion chip, the iPad 6 delivers fast and efficient performance for seamless multitasking, smooth app navigation, and immersive gaming experiences.

    Storage: The iPad 6 is available in different storage options, including 32GB and 128GB. This provides ample space to store your files, apps, and multimedia content.
    Connectivity: It supports Wi-Fi and Bluetooth connectivity, allowing you to connect to wireless networks and pair with compatible devices. Additionally, select models offer cellular connectivity for on-the-go internet access.
