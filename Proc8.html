Iphone 8

    Display: The iPhone 8 features a 4.7-inch Retina HD display with a resolution of 1334 x 750 pixels. It utilizes IPS LCD technology, delivering vibrant colors, sharpness, and wide viewing angles.
    Design: The iPhone 8 sports a glass and aluminum design, giving it a premium and sleek look. It has a compact form factor with rounded corners and a Home button with Touch ID for biometric authentication.
    Camera: The iPhone 8 comes with a 12-megapixel rear camera that captures detailed and high-quality photos. It supports optical image stabilization and has a larger sensor for improved low-light performance. The front-facing camera is a 7-megapixel camera for selfies and video calls.
    Performance: Powered by the A11 Bionic chip, the iPhone 8 delivers fast and smooth performance. It can handle demanding tasks, augmented reality applications, and graphically intensive games with ease. The device runs on iOS, Apple's operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 8 is available in different storage capacities, including 64GB and 256GB. It does not support expandable storage, so the built-in storage cannot be expanded using a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 8 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also offers 4G LTE connectivity for fast internet speeds.
    Battery: The iPhone 8 is equipped with a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery. It provides all-day battery life for typical usage and supports fast charging and wireless charging.
