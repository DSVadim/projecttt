Ipad Air

    Display: iPad Air features a stunning 10.9-inch Liquid Retina display with True Tone technology. It has a resolution of 2360 x 1640 pixels, offering sharp and vibrant visuals with excellent color accuracy.
    Design: The iPad Air showcases a sleek and modern design with a slim profile and narrow bezels. It features a premium aluminum chassis and comes in various attractive colors. The Touch ID fingerprint sensor is integrated into the top button.
    Performance: Powered by the advanced A14 Bionic chip with Neural Engine, the iPad Air delivers impressive performance and efficiency. It can handle demanding tasks, run graphics-intensive apps and games smoothly, and support augmented reality experiences.
    Camera: The iPad Air is equipped with a 12-megapixel rear camera, capable of capturing high-resolution photos and recording 4K videos. It also has a 7-megapixel front-facing camera for FaceTime calls and selfies.
    Storage: It offers ample storage options, including 64GB and 256GB, providing plenty of space for your files, apps, and media content.
    Connectivity: The iPad Air supports Wi-Fi and optional cellular connectivity, allowing you to stay connected wherever you go. It also supports Apple Pencil (2nd generation) and Magic Keyboard for enhanced productivity and creativity.
    Battery Life: The iPad Air features a long-lasting battery that can provide up to 10 hours of web browsing, video playback, or other typical usage scenarios. It also supports fast charging via USB-C for quick power top-ups.
