Iphone 7+

    Display: The iPhone 7 Plus features a 5.5-inch Retina HD display with a resolution of 1920 x 1080 pixels. The display offers excellent color reproduction, sharpness, and wide viewing angles.
    Design: The iPhone 7 Plus has an elegant and sleek design with an aluminum unibody construction. It features a solid build quality, rounded corners, and a seamless integration of hardware and software.
    Camera: The iPhone 7 Plus boasts a dual-camera system with two 12-megapixel rear cameras. The dual cameras provide optical zoom, portrait mode, and enhanced low-light photography capabilities. It also has a 7-megapixel front-facing camera for high-quality selfies and video calls.
    Performance: Powered by the A10 Fusion chip, the iPhone 7 Plus delivers smooth performance and efficient multitasking. It can handle demanding apps and games with ease. The device runs on iOS, Apple's secure and user-friendly operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 7 Plus offers different storage options, including 32GB, 128GB, and 256GB variants. However, it does not support expandable storage, so you cannot expand the storage capacity with a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 7 Plus supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also provides fast 4G LTE connectivity for seamless internet browsing and media streaming.
    Battery: The iPhone 7 Plus is equipped with a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery that offers solid battery life. It provides sufficient usage time for everyday tasks and supports fast charging for quick battery replenishment.
