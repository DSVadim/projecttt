Iphone 4

    Display: The iPhone 4 features a 3.5-inch display with a resolution of 640 x 960 pixels (Retina display). The display utilizes IPS LCD technology and offers high-quality image reproduction.
    Design: The iPhone 4 has a glass and metal body. It has a rectangular shape with rounded edges and control buttons on the front and side panels.
    Camera: The primary camera has a 5-megapixel resolution with autofocus and HD 720p video recording capability. There is also a front-facing camera for video calls and selfies.
    Performance: The iPhone 4 is powered by the Apple A4 processor, which is fast and energy-efficient. It runs on iOS, Apple's operating system.
    Memory: The built-in memory capacity is available in different variants: 8, 16, or 32 GB. The iPhone 4 does not support memory expansion through a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 4 supports wireless technologies such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth. It also has 3G connectivity for fast internet access.
    Battery: The iPhone 4 features a built-in lithium-ion battery that provides sufficient battery life. Talk time can last up to 14 hours (2G) or 7 hours (3G), and standby time is up to 300 hours.
