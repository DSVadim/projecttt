Iphone 7

    Display: The iPhone 7 features a 4.7-inch Retina HD display with a resolution of 1334 x 750 pixels. It offers excellent color accuracy, brightness, and wide viewing angles, thanks to its IPS LCD technology.
    Design: The iPhone 7 has a sleek and compact design with an aluminum unibody construction. It features a seamless design with no visible antenna lines on the back, giving it a clean and premium look.
    Camera: The iPhone 7 boasts a 12-megapixel rear camera with optical image stabilization, autofocus, and a larger aperture for improved low-light performance. It supports 4K video recording and offers features like Live Photos and HDR. The front-facing camera is a 7-megapixel camera with Retina Flash for better selfies.
    Performance: Equipped with the powerful A10 Fusion chip, the iPhone 7 delivers fast and efficient performance. It can handle demanding tasks, multitasking, and graphics-intensive apps and games with ease. The device runs on iOS, Apple's operating system.
    Storage: The iPhone 7 is available in different storage capacities, including 32GB, 128GB, and 256GB. It does not support expandable storage, so the available storage cannot be expanded with a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 7 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, NFC (for Apple Pay), and GPS. It also has 4G LTE capability for fast internet browsing and download speeds.
    Battery: The iPhone 7 is equipped with a non-removable lithium-ion battery. It offers decent battery life, providing up to 14 hours of talk time on 3G, up to 13 hours of internet use on Wi-Fi, and up to 40 hours of audio playback.
