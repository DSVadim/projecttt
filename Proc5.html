Iphone 5

    Display: The iPhone 5 boasts a 4-inch Retina display with a resolution of 1136 x 640 pixels. It offers excellent color reproduction, sharpness, and clarity, thanks to its IPS LCD technology.
    Design: The iPhone 5 features a sleek and slim design with an aluminum and glass construction. It has a rectangular shape with rounded corners and a unibody design.
    Camera: The iPhone 5 is equipped with an 8-megapixel rear camera that captures detailed photos and records Full HD 1080p videos. It also has a front-facing 1.2-megapixel camera for selfies and FaceTime video calls.
    Performance: Powered by the Apple A6 chip, the iPhone 5 delivers fast and smooth performance. It runs on iOS, Apple's operating system, which provides a seamless and intuitive user experience.
    Storage: The iPhone 5 is available in different storage capacities, including 16GB, 32GB, and 64GB. It does not support expandable storage, so the available storage cannot be expanded with a memory card.
    Connectivity: The iPhone 5 supports various connectivity options, including Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, and GPS. It also has 4G LTE capability for faster internet browsing and download speeds.
    Battery: The iPhone 5 is equipped with a built-in rechargeable lithium-ion battery that offers decent battery life. It provides up to 8 hours of talk time on 3G, up to 10 hours of video playback, and up to 40 hours of audio playback.
